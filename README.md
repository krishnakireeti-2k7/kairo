# Kairo

**Kairo** is a medical symptom logging and consultation support app designed to help people track their health over time and communicate more effectively with healthcare professionals.

The app allows users to record symptoms, analyze patterns, and generate structured reports that can be shared during doctor visits.

Instead of relying on memory, users can maintain a consistent and structured health timeline.

---

## Why Kairo?

Many patients struggle to accurately recall their symptoms during medical consultations. Important details such as frequency, severity, and triggers are often forgotten.

Kairo solves this by providing a structured system for logging symptoms and analyzing patterns over time.

The goal is not to diagnose, but to help users organize their medical history and have more productive conversations with healthcare professionals.

---

## Features

* Symptom logging with severity tracking
* Timeline view of symptom history
* Context tagging (sleep, stress, meals, etc.)
* AI-assisted pattern analysis
* Medical consultation report generation
* Secure and private health data storage
